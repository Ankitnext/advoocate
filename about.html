About us

DIRECTOR, AND ASSOCIATES OF THE FIRM

Mr. Arvind Kumar

Director

"With over two decades of experience in the legal profession, he has had the privilege of representing numerous clients in high-stakes litigation across various judicial forums, including the Hon’ble Supreme Court of India, the Hon’ble High Courts, specialized tribunals, including the Debt Recovery Tribunal and Debt Recovery Appellate Tribunal, the National Company Law Tribunal and National Company Law Appellate Tribunal, and both the State Consumer Commission and National Consumer Disputes Redressal Commission etc. His practice areas encompass a broad spectrum of legal disciplines, such as Criminal Litigation, Arbitration, Dispute Resolution, Contract Law, and Environmental Law, among others. He serves as the Standing Counsel for the Uttar Pradesh Pollution Control Board before the Hon’ble Supreme Court of India, the Hon’ble High Court of Delhi at New Delhi, and the Hon’ble National Green Tribunal. Additionally, he holds the position of Senior Panel Counsel for the Government of Uttar Pradesh before the Hon’ble Supreme Court of India. His expertise extends to acting as Special Counsel for the Government of Uttarakhand at the Hon’ble High Court of Uttarakhand at Nainital. He also represents esteemed private clients, including CEAT Ltd, ReyChem RPG, Axis Securities Ltd., and other prominent companies, ensuring robust and dedicated legal advocacy.
